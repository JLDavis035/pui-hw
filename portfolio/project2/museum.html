Pittsburgh Children's Museum

UX Designer | 2023

Problem:

How do you balance the excitement of a children's museum while still making it accessible for parents and teachers?

Design Solution:

My main focus was creating a clear, concise layout that is easy to navigate while still maintaining a whimsical air for kids. I paid great detail to the information architecture, putting the most important information like buying tickets, exhibits, and events on the landing page. Less vital information like educational resources and an about page can be accessed at the top of the nav bar. Finally, I used vibrant colors and rounded shapes to make the website appear more kid-friendly.
